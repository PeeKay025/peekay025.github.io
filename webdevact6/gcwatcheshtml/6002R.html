Introduction

6002R - Grand Complications

Manual winding

Patek Philippe gives the Reference 6002 Sky Moon Tourbillon a new look by combining the warm glow of rose gold with a Grand Feu brown enamel decoration. The double-faced case, crowns, slide piece, hour/minute hands and fold-over clasp are entirely hand-engraved with volutes and arabesques. The front dial combines Grand Feu champlevé and cloisonné enameling. This exceptional timepiece, the second most complicated Patek Philippe wristwatch (12 complications), is delivered with a pair of matching cufflinks.
Watch
Mechanical manually wound movement. Caliber R TO 27 QR SID LU CL. Sky Moon Tourbillon. Double-faced wristwatch. Minute repeater with “cathedral” gongs. Tourbillon. Front side: Perpetual calendar with retrograde date hand. Day, month, leap year in apertures. Hours and minutes of mean solar time. Moon phases. Reverse side: sidereal time, sky chart, phases and orbit of the moon.
Dial
Grand Feu champlevé and cloisonné brown enamel, gold applied Roman numerals. 18K gold dial plate.
Case
Rose gold. Hand-engraved case, lugs, slide piece and crowns. Diameter: 44 mm. Height: 17.35 mm. Humidity- and dust-protected only (not water resistant). Sapphire crystal case back.
Strap
Alligator leather with square scales, hand-stitched, chestnut patinated. Hand-engraved fold-over clasp.
