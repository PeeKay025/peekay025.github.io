5212A - Complications

Self-winding

A new complication in a steel case
Patek Philippe introduces a new complication to its calendar watches: the weekly calendar, a semi-integrated mechanism displaying the current week number, in addition to the day and date. A particularly useful feature for the modern businessman.
This steel Calatrava Weekly Calendar also houses a completely new self-winding movement with optimized performance. The dial is notable for its exclusive letters and numerals which authentically reproduce handwritten characters.
Watch
Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber 26‑330 S C J SE. Weekly calendar. Day and week number by hands. Date in an aperture. Sweep seconds hand.
Dial
Silvery opaline, blackened gold applied hour markers.
Case
Steel. Diameter: 40 mm. Height: 10.79 mm. Water-resistant to 30 m. Sapphire crystal case back.
Strap
Calfskin, hand-stitched, light brown. Prong buckle.
