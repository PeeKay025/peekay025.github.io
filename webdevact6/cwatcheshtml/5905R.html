Introduction

5905R - Complications

Self-winding

Representing a combination of two popular Patek Philippe complications, the self-winding flyback chronograph with Annual Calendar, Reference 5905, comes in a new rose gold version with a sunburst blue dial – a modern and elegant color accentuating the timeless style of this flagship model.
The patented Annual Calendar automatically takes into account 30- and 31-day months, thus requiring only one correction per year, at the end of February.
The rose gold case is distinguished by its sophisticated construction, with a concave bezel and hollowed flanks.
Watch
Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber CH 28‑520 QA 24H. Flyback chronograph. Central chronograph hand. 60-minute counter. Annual Calendar. Day, date, month and day/night indication displayed in apertures.
Dial
Sunburst blue with gold applied faceted baton-style hour markers.
Case
Rose gold. Diameter: 42 mm. Height: 14.03 mm. Water-resistant to 30 m. Sapphire crystal case back.
Strap
Alligator leather with square scales, hand-stitched, shiny dark blue. Prong buckle.
