Introduction

5740/1G - Nautilus

Self-winding

The first Grand Complication in the Nautilus collection, the Reference 5740/1 Nautilus perpetual calendar, offers a distinctive blend of sports appeal and technical sophistication. Thanks to the famous ultra-thin self-winding caliber 240 Q movement, the case (water resistant to 30 m) is remarkably svelte, making this watch Patek Philippe’s thinnest perpetual calendar. The bracelet is equipped with a new fold-over clasp providing optimized security.
Watch
Ultra-thin mechanical self-winding movement. Caliber 240 Q. Day, date, month, leap year and 24-hour indication by hands.
Dial
Blue sunburst, gold applied hour markers with luminescent coating.
Case
White gold. Diameter (10-4 o’clock): 40 mm. Height: 8.42 mm. Water-resistant to 30 m. Screw-down crown. Sapphire crystal case back.
Bracelet
White gold. Patented fold-over clasp with lockable adjustment system.
