Introduction

5811/1G - Nautilus

Self-winding

As the reinterpretation of a design icon, the Ref. 5811/1G-001 Nautilus features a case and bracelet in white gold as well as a blue sunburst dial with a black gradation to the periphery.
Water-resistant to 30 m and with a slightly enlarged diameter to 41 mm, the watch reflects its affinity with the original Nautilus of 1976 with a two-part case. It also features several subtle technical refinements and patented innovations: a secured fold-over clasp, a new lockable adjustment system, and a new pull-out piece lever system that replaces the split stem.
Watch
Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber 26‑330 S C. Date in an aperture. Sweep seconds. Dial
Dial
Sunburst blue with black-gradient rim, horizontally embossed, gold applied hour markers with luminescent coating.
Case
White gold. Diameter (10–4 o’clock): 41 mm. Height: 8.2 mm. Water-resistant to 30 m. Screw-down crown. Sapphire crystal case back.
Bracelet
White gold. Patented fold-over clasp with lockable adjustment system.
