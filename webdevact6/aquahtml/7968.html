Introduction

7968/300R - Aquanaut

Self-winding

Patek Philippe has launched its first self-winding ladies’ chronograph – it is the first chronograph in the sporty-chic, contemporary Aquanaut Luce collection.
The Ref. 7968/300R-001 Aquanaut Luce “Rainbow” chronograph in rose gold was created especially for modern, active women. It features a rainbow consisting of baguette diamonds and multi-colored sapphire that are invisibly set.
The unique combination of spirited sporty looks and the jeweler’s art is delivered with three ultra-resistant composite straps, one each in red, matte beige, and matte white.
Watch
Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber CH 28‑520. Flyback chronograph. Central chronograph hand. 60-minute counter.
Dial
White mother-of-pearl, engraved with Aquanaut pattern, baguette multicolored sapphire hour markers and gold applied numerals.
Case
Rose gold. Diameter (10–4 o’clock): 39.9 mm. Height: 10.37 mm. Water-resistant to 30 m. Sapphire crystal case back.
Gemsetting
40 multicolored sapphires: 2.31 cts. (bezel). 40 diamonds: 2.05 cts. (bezel). 12 multicolored sapphires: 0.31 ct. (hour markers). 10 diamonds: 0.36 ct. (fold-over clasp). Total of 52 baguette-cut multicolored sapphires (2.62 cts) and 50 baguette-cut diamonds (2.41 cts).
Strap
Composite material, red (originally fitted). Composite material, matte beige and matte white (additional straps). Diamond-set patented fold-over clasp.
