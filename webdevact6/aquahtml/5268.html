Introduction

5268/461G - Aquanaut

Self-winding

Patek Philippe combines "contemporary casual chic" design and Haute Joaillerie in a new white gold Aquanaut Luce model.
The dial center is paved with baguette-cut diamonds and blue sapphires forming a checkerboard pattern recalling the Aquanaut collection’s emblematic pattern. The chapter ring alternates between baguette-cut blue sapphire hour-markers and snow-set brilliant-cut diamonds.
The rounded octagonal bezel is enhanced by a row of baguette-cut sapphires set in a subtle gradient running from light to dark blue. The chromatic harmony is echoed by the integrated strap in dark blue composite material.
This watch is equipped with self-winding 26-330 S caliber, visible through a sapphire crystal case-back.
Watch
Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber 26‑330 S. Sweep seconds.
Dial
Paved with baguette-cut diamonds and sapphires, baguette-cut sapphire hour markers, and hour ring set with diamonds (snow setting). 18K gold dial plate.
Case
White gold. Diameter (10 - oclock): 38.8 mm. Thickness: 9.1 mm. Water-resistant to 30 m. Screw-down crown. Sapphire crystal case back.
Gemsetting
Bezel: 40 baguette sapphires, light to dark blue gradient (3.6 cts). Lugs: 8 baguette diamonds (0.58 ct). Dial: 20 baguette sapphires (1.13 ct). Dial: 20 baguette diamonds (1.12 ct). Hour markers: 12 baguette sapphires (0.56 ct). Hour ring: 160 diamonds (0.71 ct). Clasp: 10 baguette diamonds (0.33 ct). Total of 72 baguette-cut sapphires (5.29 cts), 38 baguette-cut diamonds (2.03 cts) and 160 brilliant-cut diamonds (0.71 ct).
Strap
Composite material, dark blue. Patented fold-over clasp in white gold.
